I am a PhD in political science. My major studies are (1) public policy analysis and administration, and (2) studies of criminal justice. Additionally, I have 19 years of working experience, building a career in the public services, lately as a manager and advisor. I have used my data analysis skills in my work in order to improve public policies. Then I have decided to combine my expertise with computer sciences, after I came to the Netherlands.

During my learning process since 2019, I have completed a number of projects in data science and data analytics, such as predictions related to whether and payments, creating dashboards to follow key performance indicators (e.g., sales, profits, and shipments), visualization of data for transportation services, mapping of violence/crime and political opinions, collecting data via web-scraping, and application of machine learning models to business problems.

I love coding and programming with Python and producing easily understandable outputs for clients and decision makers. In writing reports, I prefer simplicity to increase innovative thinking and to improve decision-making. Currently I have been seeking job opportunities to apply my research skills.
